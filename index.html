Welcome to Swat Valley

The Switzerland of Pakistan

Swat is a majestic valley in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. Known for its breathtaking mountains, rivers, and warm people, Swat is the perfect place for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Top Attractions

Malam Jabba

Malam Jabba is a famous ski resort known for snowboarding and winter activities.

Kalam Valley

Kalam is a scenic valley with pine forests, calm rivers, and peaceful vibes.

Mahodand Lake

This alpine lake is perfect for boating, fishing, and enjoying natural beauty.

Fizagat Park

A riverside park near Mingora with picnic spots and walking tracks.

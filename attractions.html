Kalam valley

Kalam Valley is a peaceful and scenic valley in the upper Swat region. Surrounded by pine forests and snow-capped mountains, Kalam is known for its fresh air, calm rivers, and relaxing atmosphere. It is an ideal place for camping, sightseeing, and enjoying the serenity of nature.

Mahodand lake

Mahodand Lake is a beautiful alpine lake located near Kalam. Surrounded by tall mountains and pine forests, it is a popular spot for boating, fishing, and horse riding. The crystal-clear water and breathtaking scenery make it a must-visit destination in Swat.

Fizagat park

Fizagat Park is a riverside recreational park near Mingora, perfect for families and kids. It has playgrounds, walking tracks, and picnic spots with views of the Swat River. The cool breeze and scenic surroundings make it a relaxing place to spend quality time with loved ones
